Damages

At and Below Zero (0) HP
  • Collapses and falls into a coma.
  • Length of time in coma to survive without treatment: P.E. hours
  • If the player rolls 2 successful recovery rolls out of 3, the character breaks out of the coma and is no longer in danger of dying. This also means that he is brought up to 1 HP:­

    • Recovery Ratio (roll 2 out of 3)
    32% from an intern or nurse (R.N.)
    46% from a doctor without proper facilities
    56% from a doctor at a clinic (fair facilities)
    66% from a hospital
    70% from a major, large hospital

    Dead: HP is below negative P.E.
    Collision: 2D4 per 10 mph.
    Falling: 1D6 damage per 10 feet.
    Saving Throws
  • Curses: 15+
  • Disease: 14+
  • Poison (lethal): 14+
  • Poison (non-lethal): 16+
  • Harmful Drugs: 15+
  • Insanity: 12+
  • Magic: 12+ to 16+ (depends on the power level of the spell caster)
  • Magic (ritual): 16+
  • Psionics:
    • 6+ for Psi-Stalkers
    10+ for Master Psychic (including Mind Melters, Dog Boys, and Bursters)
    12+ for Major and Minor Psychics
    15+ for ordinary people and animals

    Strikes
    Hand Strike Damage
  • Backhand: 1D4
  • Backhand (Martial Arts): 1D6
  • Back Flip: 1D6
  • Punch: 1D4
  • Punch (Karate/Martial Arts): 2D4
  • Elbow/Forearm: 1D6
  • Power Punch: Does double damage plus any other damage bo­nuses, but counts as 2 melee attacks. Applicable to all hand strikes.
    • Foot Strike Damage
  • Kick: 1D8 (or 2D4)
  • Karate Kick: 2D6
  • Leap Kick: 3D8, but counts as 2 melee attacks/actions.
  • Knee: 1D6
  • Backward Sweep: No damage, but knocks down opponent if strike is successful (he loses 1 melee action and initiative).
  • Trip/Leg Hook: No damage, but knocks down opponent if the strike is successful (he loses 1 melee action and initiative).
  • Power Kick: Does double damage, but counts as 2 melee attacks and cannot be done with a Leap Kick.

    • Thrown/Dropped Small Objects: 1D4 or 1D6.
    Thrown/Dropped Large Objects: 3D6 per 100 lbs.
