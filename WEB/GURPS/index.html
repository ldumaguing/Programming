Attack/Action Roll

ES: Effective Skill level plus modifiers
Roll: 3d6
Notes:
• Roll vs. Roll
• Ties may happen. By default, no changes to situations, conditions, etc.
• Ties may be broken by comparing the degrees of success.
• Target may roll for defense if aware of the attack.
• Target may roll for defense if the attack was a ordinary success.
• See pages 556 and 557 for critical results.
• Of course, target must be within range or reach in order to attack.

Roll Results (Roll vs. ES)
Summary: Roll low to succeed.
• Roll 3 or 4: Crit-Success.
• Roll 18: Crit-Fail.
• Roll <= ES:
• (ES - Roll) >= 10: Crit-Success.
• else: Success....
• Roll 17 and (ES <= 15): Crit-Fail.
• (Roll - ES) >= 10: Crit-Fail.
• else: Fail.

Damage Roll Steps

1. Basic Damage: A weapon's damage roll.
2. If the target is at or beyond half of a weapon's max distance, reduce Basic Damage by half round down.
3. Penatrating Damage: Basic Damage minus Damage Resistance (DR). DR may be more than one to further reduce Basic Damage.
4. Divide Penatrating Damage by Armor Divisor round down. Minimum of zero (0).
5. Wounding Modifier: Any Penatrating Damage left is multiplied by this modifier which depends on the weapon's damage type.
6. Reduce target's HP by the final calculated damage.

Options

• Each armor's level of Hardened reduces the Armor Divisor of an attack by one step. These steps are, in order: “ignores DR,” 100, 10, 5, 3, 2, and 1 (no divisor).
