Attack/Action Roll

ES: Effective Skill level plus modifiers
Roll: 3d6
Notes:
• (Roll vs. ES) vs. (Roll vs. ES)
• Ties may happen. By default, no changes to situations, conditions, etc.
• Ties may be broken by comparing the degrees of success.
• Target may roll for defense if aware of the attack.
• Target may roll for defense if the attack was a ordinary success.
• See pages 556 and 557 for critical results.

Damage Steps

1. Basic Damage: A weapon's damage roll.
2. Penatrating Damage: Basic Damage minus total Damage Resistance (DR). Minimum of zero (0).
3. Damage: Penatrating Damage multiplied by the Wounding Modifier.
4. Reduce target's HP by the Damage.

Options

▶ If the target is at or beyond half of a weapon's max distance, reduce Basic Damage by half round down.
▶ Divide Penatrating Damage by the weapon's Armor Divisor round down. Minimum of zero (0).
▶ Each armor's level of Hardened reduces the weapon's Armor Divisor of an attack by one step. These steps are, in order: “ignores DR,” 100, 10, 5, 3, 2, and 1 (no divisor).
▶ Apply any critical results.
